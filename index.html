About

From a young age, pencils, crayons, and colorful paper riddled my workspace, yearning for my small hands to experiment and let my imagination shine on the pieces of paper. I found great joy in expressing my creativity and creating unique works of art which influenced me to hold visual art as a hobby through my childhood, teenage years, and now into adulthood. Visual art has always been a major influence in my life as I grew up. It helped me express my creativity, while also giving me a sense of accomplishment when I was able to complete a work of art. It also allowed me to express myself, as I was able to create pieces that reflected my own unique style and vision. It was a way for me to express my emotions, as I could create pieces that were reflective of my inner thoughts and feelings. Art also allowed me to explore my creativity, as I was able to experiment with different techniques and mediums to create works of art that were truly unique and personal. I found peace in creating art. It was a form of self-expression that allowed me to express my emotions in a way that words couldn't.
